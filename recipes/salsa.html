Salsa Best Recipe

Lets walkthrough the steps to make the best salsa!

Salsa is a vibrant and high-energy social dance originating from the Caribbean, primarily Cuba and Puerto Rico, before exploding in popularity in New York City during the 1960s and 70s. Characterized by its infectious eight-count rhythm and intricate footwork, it blends African drum beats with Spanish melodic influences. Whether it's the fast-paced spins of "LA Style" or the circular flow of "Casino," salsa is all about the connection between partners and the joy of spontaneous musical expression

Ingedients List

    • 4 large ripe tomatoes, chopped
      1 onion, chopped
      1 tomatillo, diced (Optional)
      ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
      3 cloves garlic, minced
      1 tablespoon lime juice
      salt to taste
      1 jalapeño pepper, minced

    • Steps

    • Gather all ingredients.
      Combine tomatoes, onion, tomatillo, cilantro, garlic, lime juice, and salt in a medium-sized mixing bowl. Mix well.
      Add 1/2 of the jalapeño pepper and taste. If you desire your salsa with more kick, add remaining 1/2 jalapeño.
      Cover the salsa and chill until ready to serve.
